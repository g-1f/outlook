Trading Model Architecture Details

Stage 1: Supervised Fine-Tuning

This stage focuses on building and optimizing reasoning capabilities:

  • Data Collection - Gathering data from GIR, market sources, and news feeds
  • Reasoning Trajectory Generation - Creating synthetic chain-of-thought reasoning paths that analyze market conditions
  • Trajectory Evaluation & Selection - Assessing the quality of different reasoning approaches and selecting top performers
  • Model Fine-Tuning - Optimizing the model to follow effective reasoning patterns for market analysis

Stage 2: Reinforcement Learning

This stage implements a reinforcement learning approach to optimize trading decisions:

  • Event-Driven RL Environment - A simulation environment that reacts to market events and news intake
  • PPO/GRPO Training - Training using Proximal Policy Optimization or Guided Policy Optimization algorithms
  • Reward Functions:
    • Format - Evaluating the structure and format of decisions
    • Return - Measuring financial performance
    • Penalize - Applying penalties for high-risk or unfavorable actions
  • Trading Decision Output - Generating final portfolio weights and trading actions
